Volleyball of the Midwest


About

Volleyball of the Midwest was created to support and enhance the local volleyball community. The idea is simple, a community of volleyball enthusiasts supporting future generations of volleyball enthusiasts, for the players by the players. Our goal is to run tournaments and leagues in order to support camps for youth players and help those individuals supplement the high costs of club sports.

By participating in Volleyball of the Midwest events and activities you help support future generations of volleyball players to come.

So, thank you for your time and contributions, we could not have done this without you.

League Information

Leagues games start at 6:00 pm sharp, must show up by early to set up the court
If teams do not show up by 6 the opposing team my write a forfeit for late/absent team
Teams will play two matches a night or till dark
Scoring will be Rally scoring
Matches will be best of 3 sets win by 2 cap 27 points with the 3rd set being played to 15 no cap
If the ball contacts the boundary line the ball is considered in
If the ball contacts the net on a serve it is considered live and is playable
Any contact with the net by a player is considered a foul, the play is dead and a point is awarded to the team who did not commit the foul. (Hair is the only exception, and is able to come in contact with the net)
Players must right down the name of their subs on the sub sheet
D1- Overhand sets need to have little to no spin on the ball (1-2 rotations)
D2- Overhand sets are nearly clean with slight spin (able to 2-5 rotations)
D3- Overhand sets can have spin but should not be out of control
All games are self-governed, please call your own fouls and do not make the other team call them on you
Any discrepancy will result in a redo

Specific Rules for 4's

No sets over the net
No open hand tips
All players are live and may spike the ball (no down player)
